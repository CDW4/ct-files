🌅 Breakfast
Choose one daily — rotate
🍳Brain Omelette + Toast
3 eggs, spinach, garlic, bell pepper, EVOO. Avocado + 2 brazil nuts on side. 8 min.
🥣Grits / Oatmeal / Malt-O-Meal
Cooked in goat milk. Always add: honey, walnuts, 2 brazil nuts, pumpkin seeds. Cooker adds their touch.
🫙Greek Yogurt Parfait EASIEST
Chobani full fat plain → berries → walnuts + 2 brazil nuts → honey → pumpkin seeds. 3 min zero cooking.
🫘Black Beans, Rice & Eggs
Gallo pinto — Blue Zone staple. Batch cook Sunday. Reheat 3 min + fresh eggs. Avocado on side.
🐟Saltfish, Bread & Mango CULTURAL
Caribbean staple. Batch cook saltfish Sunday — keeps 4 days. Reheat + toast + fresh mango. 5 min morning.
🥤Kachava Smoothie MAX 2x/WK
Low appetite days only. Goat milk + frozen banana + spinach. Check label vs supplement stack. 2 min.
🍵 ALWAYS SERVE WITH
Green tea (morning only) OR herbal tea (chamomile/rooibos) + warm water with lemon on waking
☀️ Lunch
Biggest meal — planned weekly
🥣Lentil Veggie Soup HERO ⭐
Batch Sunday. Lentils, sweet potato, spinach, broccoli, bone broth, turmeric+pepper, EVOO, lemon. Reheat 3 min.
🍗Baked Wings + Roasted Veg STAPLE ⭐
She always eats this. EVOO, garlic herb, 400°F 35–40 min. Start at shift start. Sweet potato same pan.
🐟Sardine & Veggie Bowl
B12 + DHA from food. Blend into soup if she resists. Otherwise over sweet potato + broccoli + EVOO lemon.
🍲Turkey Veggie Soup
Ground turkey, carrots, celery, kale, bone broth, turmeric. Batch Sunday. Rotate vegetables weekly.
🍗Chicken & Sweet Potato Bowl EASIEST
Rotisserie chicken (bought Sunday) + pre-roasted sweet potato + sautéed spinach. 10 min. Zero cooking skill needed.
📦 BATCH COOK SUNDAY
Lentil soup • Turkey soup • Sweet potato cubes • Rotisserie chicken • Black beans + rice • Saltfish
🌙 Dinner
Lighter than lunch — post weekly
🐟Fish Soup 2x/WEEK ⭐
Salmon or cod, asparagus, bone broth, garlic, turmeric, lemon. UTI + brain + insulin in one bowl. 20 min.
🍗Wings + Roasted Veg STAPLE ⭐
Same wings. Rotate vegetables nightly — broccoli, beets, cauliflower, asparagus, Brussels sprouts. Never repeat.
🐟Baked Salmon + Greens
Salmon, lemon, garlic, EVOO. 375°F 15 min. Garlic kale + roasted asparagus on side. Omega-3 powerhouse.
🍲Lentil & Beet Stew
Lentils, beets, carrots, garlic, bone broth. Beets boost brain blood flow. Warm grain bread for comfort.
🫑Stuffed Bell Peppers
Ground turkey, quinoa, spinach, garlic in halved peppers. Bake 25 min. Colorful — dementia patients respond to colorful food.
🍤Shrimp & Cauliflower Rice
Shrimp (zinc naturally), cauliflower rice, garlic, EVOO, lemon. Light on digestion. 15 min.
🌛 NIGHTTIME — EVERY NIGHT
Warm goat milk + honey ⭐ OR chamomile tea (1 bag). Small oatmeal if hungry. Non-negotiable sleep signal.
