Quick Reheat Reference
🥣 All Soups
Pot on low-medium heat, 4–5 minutes. Stir occasionally. Do NOT boil. Finish with EVOO + lemon.
🍗 Wings
Oven 350°F 10 min or air fryer 5 min. Never microwave — skin goes soggy. No added sauces.
🍽️ Everything Else
Microwave 90–120 seconds covered with damp paper towel. EVOO drizzle + lemon after reheating.
🚫 Never Give
Sugar, fast food, fried food, store wing sauces, canola oil. Symptoms return immediately.
💧 Always Offer
Water with every meal. Coconut water mid-afternoon. Warm goat milk + honey at 7:45pm every night.
🌰 Always Serve
2 brazil nuts beside breakfast every morning. Selenium from food — replaces a supplement entirely.
