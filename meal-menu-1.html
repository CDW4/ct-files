Brain Omelette + Toast
Universal • 8 min
🧠 Brain ⚡ Quick
▼
3 eggs, fresh spinach, garlic, diced bell pepper, EVOO in the pan. Fold and serve with whole grain toast and avocado slices. 2 brazil nuts on the side every time.

Black Beans, Rice & Eggs
Costa Rica Blue Zone • Batch cook
🧠 Brain 🌍 Blue Zone 📦 Batch Cook
▼
Gallo pinto style — the breakfast eaten by one of the longest-lived populations on earth. Beans and rice cooked Sunday, reheated in minutes. Eggs fried or scrambled fresh on top.
Sunday prep: Cook large pot of black beans with garlic, onion, cumin, EVOO. Cook brown rice. Refrigerate both separately.
Morning: Reheat beans + rice in pan 3 min, fry or scramble 2 eggs, serve together. Add sliced avocado and hot sauce if tolerated.
Plant protein Fiber Iron B12 from eggs Potassium from avocado

Saltfish, Bread & Mango
Caribbean • Jamaica/Trinidad • Batch cook
🌍 Caribbean 📦 Batch Cook ⭐ Cultural Staple
▼
Saltfish sautéed with onion, tomato, garlic, scotch bonnet (optional), EVOO. Served with hard dough bread or whole grain toast and fresh mango slices. Culturally familiar and deeply nourishing.
Sunday batch: Soak saltfish overnight to remove excess salt. Boil 10 min, flake apart. Sauté with onion, tomato, garlic, EVOO. Refrigerate up to 4 days.
Morning: Reheat saltfish 3 min. Toast bread. Slice fresh mango. Serve together.
B12 from saltfish Omega-3 Vitamin C from mango Potassium from mango

Food Rules
Symptoms flared when sugar, fast food, and processed items returned after the antibiotic period. The liquid diet proved her body responds to food quality. These rules protect that.
🚫 Never Give Her These
×Refined sugar or candy
×Fast food of any kind
×Fried food
×Seed oils (canola, vegetable)
×Processed snacks / chips
×Sugary drinks or juice
×Caffeine after 2pm
×Heavy meals close to bed
×Artificial additives
×White bread or pastries
✅ Always Cook With
→EVOO — never vegetable or canola oil
→Bone broth as soup base always
→Garlic and onion as base of every savory dish
→Turmeric + black pepper in every soup
→Lemon finish on soups and greens
→Goat milk instead of cow milk where possible
→2 brazil nuts beside every breakfast plate
→Pumpkin seeds on oatmeal every time
